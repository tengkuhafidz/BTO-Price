About

We developed this simple web app simply because we were struggling to digest all the relevant information regarding securing our 1st BTO. Hence, we decided to break down the information to smaller bits, and display it in a way that will help us understand it better.

Hence this web app. We initially wanted to develop this for our personal use, but then we thought - heck, might as well publish it cos others might benefit too.

Feel free to share this around. Enjoy!
